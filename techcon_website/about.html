Our History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small developer meetup and has grown into a leading global technology conference. Over the years, it expanded to include AI, cybersecurity, cloud computing, robotics, and startup innovation. After successfully hosting virtual events during the pandemic, TechCon now welcomes thousands of participants worldwide and continues to be a hub for technological advancement and collaboration.

Our Mission

To provide a vibrant and unparalleled learning experience with top-notch topics and speakers for utility professionals, focusing on practical technical knowledge and industry networking.
